PHASE 3 — Define Business Questions

Before touching the data we define analysis questions.

This is extremely important.

The project story will be:

"Analyze sales performance and identify growth opportunities."

Questions we will answer:

1️⃣ What are the monthly revenue trends?

2️⃣ Which products generate the most profit?

3️⃣ Which regions underperform?

4️⃣ Which customer segments drive revenue?

5️⃣ What are the top 10 customers?

6️⃣ How do discounts impact profit?

These questions simulate real business analysis tasks.
