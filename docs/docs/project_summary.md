# Sales Analytics Insights

## Dataset
Global Superstore dataset containing 51,000+ orders across multiple regions and product categories.

## Key Business Insights

### 1. Technology drives the highest revenue
Technology products generate the largest share of total sales and profit compared to Furniture and Office Supplies.

### 2. Consumer segment contributes most revenue
The Consumer customer segment accounts for more than half of total sales.

### 3. Sales show strong seasonal growth
Monthly sales trends show increasing revenue throughout the year, with peaks during the last quarter.

### 4. Regional performance varies
Some regions generate strong revenue but comparatively lower profit margins.

### 5. Product performance varies widely
Certain sub-categories generate high sales but relatively low profit, indicating potential pricing or discounting issues.
